Ingredients

Here are the ingredients to prepare lasagna.

  • 500 g of minced meat (beef or a mixture)
  • 9 sheets of lasagna
  • 400 g of tomato sauce
  • 250 g of ricotta cheese
  • 200 g of mozzarella (shredded)
  • 50 g of parmesan (grated)
  • Onion, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper
  • Steps

Steps

Follow these steps to prepare your dish.

  1. In a pan, sauté the onion and garlic in olive oil. Add the meat and cook.
  2. Add the tomato sauce, season with salt and pepper, and let it simmer.
  3. In a baking dish, alternate layers of lasagna, meat, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  4. Finish with a layer of lasagna, tomato sauce, and mozzarella.
  5. Sprinkle with parmesan and bake at 180 °C (350 °F) for 30-35 minutes.
