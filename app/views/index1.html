Ingredients

Here are the ingredients to prepare chicken curry.

  • 500 g of chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 400 ml of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of curry paste
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Steps

Steps

Follow these steps to prepare your dish.

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan. Add the onion and garlic, saut√© until golden brown.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until golden.
  3. Add the curry paste, mix well, then incorporate the coconut milk.
  4. Let it simmer for 15-20 minutes. Season with salt.
