Ingredients

Here are the ingredients to prepare tuna rillettes.

  • 1 can of tuna
  • 100 g of cream cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of capers
  • Pepper
  • Steps

Steps

Follow these steps to prepare your dish.

  1. Drain the tuna and flake it into a bowl.
  2. Add the cream cheese, lemon juice, capers, and pepper.
  3. Mix until you achieve a smooth consistency. Serve with toast.
