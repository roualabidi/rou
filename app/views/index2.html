Ingredients

Here are the ingredients to prepare vegetable soup.

  • 2 carrots
  • 1 leek
  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 liter of vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper
  • Steps

Steps

Follow these steps to prepare your dish.

  1. Peel and dice all the vegetables.
  2. In a pot, sauté the onion in a little olive oil.
  3. Add the other vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth and let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Blend the soup and season with salt and pepper.
