Ingredients

Here are the ingredients to prepare tiramisu.

  • 250 g of mascarpone
  • 3 eggs
  • 100 g of sugar
  • 1 cup of strong coffee
  • 20 ladyfinger biscuits
  • Cocoa powder
  • Steps

Steps

Follow these steps to prepare your dish.

  1. Separate the egg whites from the yolks. Beat the yolks with the sugar, then incorporate the mascarpone.
  2. Whip the egg whites until stiff and gently fold them into the mixture.
  3. Dip the biscuits in the coffee and arrange them in a dish.
  4. Alternate a layer of biscuits and a layer of cream, finishing with the cream.
  5. Sift cocoa powder on top and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
