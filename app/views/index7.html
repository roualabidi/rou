Ingredients

Here are the ingredients to prepare shrimp ceviche.

  • 300 g of cooked and peeled shrimp
  • 2 limes
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 tomato
  • Fresh coriander
  • 1 avocado
  • Salt, pepper
  • Steps

Steps

Follow these steps to prepare your dish.

  1. Cut the shrimp into pieces. Squeeze the juice from the limes.
  2. Chop the onion, tomato, and coriander.
  3. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl, adding salt and pepper.
  4. Let marinate in the refrigerator for 20 minutes. Serve cold with avocado slices.
