Ingredients

Here are the ingredients to prepare herb-crusted lamb chops.

  • 4 lamb chops
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Fresh thyme and rosemary
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Steps

Steps

Follow these steps to prepare your dish.

  1. Finely chop the garlic, thyme, and rosemary.
  2. Brush the lamb chops with olive oil and rub them with the herb mixture.
  3. Heat a skillet and cook the chops for 3-4 minutes on each side.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
