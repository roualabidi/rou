Ingredients

Here are the ingredients to prepare cherry clafoutis.

  • 500 g of cherries
  • 3 eggs
  • 100 g of sugar
  • 80 g of flour
  • 300 ml of milk
  • 1 packet of vanilla sugar
  • Butter for the baking dish
  • Steps

Steps

Follow these steps to prepare your dessert.

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 °C (350 °F). Butter a baking dish.
  2. In a bowl, beat the eggs with the sugar, then add the flour and vanilla sugar.
  3. Gradually incorporate the milk, mixing well.
  4. Arrange the cherries in the dish and pour the batter over them. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes.
